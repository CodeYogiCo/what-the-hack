What The Hack is this!

"What The Hack" is a platform to organize hackathons in Snapdeal. Hackathon is a way for all Snapdeal techies and non-techies to take a break and for 1 day & 1 night work together, think, code and build interesting stuff

On 4th September, 2015 we will host our first Hackathon from 3 PM - 9 AM in Gurgaon and Bangalore office. On this day everyone is welcome to code their own crazy ideas or feature or bug fix. There are some very exciting prizes to be won, along with a lot of admiration. Come join the party!
